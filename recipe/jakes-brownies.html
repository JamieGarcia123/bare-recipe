Jake's Brownies

Ooey, gooey, and oh so goody...

Cook time: 25 mins

Prep time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1/2 cup
  • Sugar: 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 tsp
  • Eggs: 2
  • Baking Soda: 1/4 tsp
  • Salt: 1/4 tsp
  • Flour: 1/2 cup
  • Cacoa Powder: 1/2 cup
  • Cream of tarter: 1/4 tsp (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350f
  2. Step 2: Grease a a 9"x9" pan with oil spray
  3. Step 3: Melt butter in a separate microwave safe container
  4. Step 4: In a medium mixing bowl whish together butter sugar and vanilla
  5. Step 5: Whisk in eggs until fully incorporated
  6. Step 6: Add dry ingredients (baking soda flour salt and cocoa powder) to the wet ingredients. Optionally you can add about a cup of chocolate chips or nuts at this step
  7. Step 7: Pour batter into the 9"x9" pan.
  8. Step 8: Bake for 25-30 minutes
  9. Step 9: Brownies will set better after cooling for 30 minutes you do not want the batter to jiggle when taking out of the oven.
