Simple Potato Wedges

Great fluffy and crispy side dish or snack! Pair with your favorit dipping sauce!

Cook time: 25 mins

Prep time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • Potato: 4 ~ each one is about 3 fingers width thick
  • salt: 3 tsp (to taste - coarser salt may need more.)
  • pepper: 1 tsp
  • olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • smoked sweet paprika: 1 1/2 tbs
  • garlic powder: 1 tbs

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat your oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Optionally line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside. OR use a large pizza pan unlined
  2. Step 2: Wedge the potatoes. Cut long ways in half then half those halves. Lastly half the now quartered potatoes. They wedges cook best about a finger width thick. One potato gets 8-10 wedges.
  3. Step 3: In a large bowl, mix together the potatoes, olive oil, and seasonings so that the potatoes are well coated. *mix in some grated parmesan too!
  4. Step 4: Place the potatoes on the prepared baking sheet with the skin side down. Baking them this way will keep you from needing to flip them half-way through baking.
  5. Step 5: Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
