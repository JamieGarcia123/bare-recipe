Advocado bagels

4 Ingredients to turn those about to go bad advocado into bagels! Easy prep and ready quicker than bagels!

Cook time: 20 mins

Prep time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • Advocado: 1 large
  • Plain Greek yogurt: 5 1/2 oz
  • All Purpose Flour: 1 3/4 cup
  • Baking Powder: 3 tsp

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper. Slightly oil the paper with cooking oil spray. Set aside.
  2. Step 2: On a chopping board, scoop out the flesh of your ripe avocado. If you see brown or black spots, remove them. Dark spots when baked add a very bitter flavor to the bagels.
  3. Step 3: Mash the green, ripe avocado flesh with a fork into a smooth puree, then pack in a measuring cup to get 1/2 cup. If you do not have enough, simply add yogurt.
  4. Step 4: Add the mashed avocado, yogurt and self-rising flour in a mixing bowl, stir with a fork, pressing to bring ingredients together.
  5. Step 5: When it starts to form pieces, lightly oil your hands and knead the dough to form a soft, consistent avocado dough. **If the dough is too wet, adjust it by kneading in more flour.
  6. Step 6: Divide it into 4 equal portions for larger bagels (as seen in the pictures above) or 6 for smaller bagels.
  7. Step 7: Divide it into 4 equal portions for larger bagels (as seen in the pictures above) or 6 for smaller bagels.
  8. Step 8: Place the bagels in the prepared baking sheet 2 thumbs away from each other – they expand in the oven.
  9. Step 9: Place a piece of foil in the bagel holes, so the holes do not shrink back in the oven.
  10. Step 10: Bake the bagels for 20-22 minutes at 350°F until golden brown.
  11. Step 11: Let them cool down on a cooling rack completely before slicing.
