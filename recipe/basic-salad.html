Basic Salad

Use whatever greens, produce, herbs, proteins... lets gooo!!

Cook time: 0 mins

Prep time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Lettuce: 1 head
  • tomatoes: 1 any
  • bell pepper: 1 any
  • cucumber: 1 any

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Chop up the lettuce and other veggies into bite size peices
  2. Step 2: If using onion or cucumber try slicing thinly.
  3. Step 3: add fresh herbs to get a little zing!
  4. Step 4: add any protein, maybe that can of bean laying around (drain and rinsed)
  5. Step 5: top with your favorite salad dressing
