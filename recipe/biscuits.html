Biscuits

Fluffy homemade biscuits in just 30 minutes!

Cook time: 12 mins

Prep time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Flour: 2 cups
  • White Sugar: 1 tbs
  • Baking powder: 1 tbs
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Cubed COLD butter: 1/2 cup
  • Cold Milk: 3/4 cup

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 425f
  2. Step 2: In a medium bowl whisk together flour/sugar/salt/baking powder thoroughly
  3. Step 3: Using a pastry knife or fork cut in the cubed butter
  4. Step 4: Keep cutting the butter into the dough until well chopped in tinier pieces through out
  5. Step 5: Slowly stir in the milk
  6. Step 6: Stir slowly until well combine and dough is sticky but leaves the side of the bowl
  7. Step 7: Lightly flour the surface of your counter or work area
  8. Step 8: Place dough onto the floured surface
  9. Step 9: Gently roll dough into a ball by using the heel of your palm
  10. Step 10: DO NOT OVER WORK - want to see the butter stay in pieces - if you think dough is too warm just place in fridge before rolling out
  11. Step 11: Once a firmer dough has been lightly kneaded roll the dough with a pin to 1" thickness
  12. Step 12: Take a 2"-3" round cutter or a round cup and press into dough
  13. Step 13: Place dough on either a cookie sheet or into a cast iron 10" pan
  14. Step 14: If using the cast iron pan dough will touch each other and fit about 6-8
  15. Step 15: If using a cookie sheet you can space out more
  16. Step 16: Cook biscuits for 10-12 minutes or until lightly golden on top. Biscuits should fluff in heigh about 2-3x what they were. If your biscuits did not fluff up the dough was worked too long but no worries they usually a little fluffy still
  17. Step 17: Enjoy immediately with jam, jelly, butter, PB, whatever you like!
